About our organisation


Youth citizen entreprenuriship helps provide aspiring entrepreneurs with the skills and funding necessary to start and/or successfully run a business. New entrepreneurs gather and exchange knowledge and business ideas with experienced entrepreneur, with whom they collaborate for a period of 1 to 6 months. The stay is partly financed by the {European Commission.}

Benefits

Those who will be succefull will receive funding of up to K850,000.00 depending on the kind of their business. They will also get a chance to recive guidance from more experienced entrepreneurs on how to run and manage a business. this aplies only to those under the age of 35.

about the programe


This is an exchange programme which gives new or aspiring entrepreneurs the funding as well as a chance to learn from experienced entrepreneur about establishing and running a business.
